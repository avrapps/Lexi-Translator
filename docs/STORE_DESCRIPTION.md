## Long Description (All Stores)

**Your language barrier ends here — no internet, no cloud, no compromises.**

Lexi is a fully offline AI translation platform that runs entirely on your device. No data leaves your phone. No servers process your conversations. Your words stay yours.

### 🎯 REAL-TIME INTERPRETER
Have natural two-way conversations across languages. Lexi listens, translates, and speaks — all in real-time. The animated AI Orb shows you exactly what's happening. Works in cafes, airports, hospitals, and remote villages with zero signal.

• Dual-language auto-detection — no button pressing needed
• Live waveform visualization during speech capture
• Conversation cards with confidence scores
• Push-to-talk or hands-free continuous modes
• Presentation mode with large captions (up to 48sp)

### 📝 TEXT TRANSLATION
Paste, type, dictate, or import documents. Get translations back in milliseconds with alternatives, dictionary info, and transliteration for foreign scripts.

• Auto-detect source language from just 3 characters
• Up to 5 alternative translation suggestions
• Dictionary with grammar, gender, usage, and formality
• Quick actions: copy, share, speak, save, compare
• Supports up to 10,000 characters per request

### 🎙️ NEURAL SPEAK (TTS STUDIO)
Convert any text or document into natural speech using multiple AI voice engines — Kokoro, Piper, and VITS. Read PDFs, EPUBs, and documents aloud.

• Adjustable speed (0.5x–3.0x), pitch (0.5x–2.0x), pause, and volume
• Export to WAV, MP3, FLAC, OGG
• Resume reading from where you left off
• Sleep timer (5–120 min) and background playback
• Voice library with preview before download
• Supports up to 500,000 characters per session

### 📚 ENCRYPTED LIBRARY
All your translations, conversations, and voice memos are stored in an AES-256 encrypted local database. Search, filter, tag, and organize with full-text search across 50,000+ entries.

• Biometric lock for sensitive content (fingerprint/face)
• Folders (3 levels deep), collections, and tags (20 per entry)
• Export to JSON, TXT, CSV, PDF, or audio
• Bulk operations: multi-select delete, move, tag, export
• Time filters: Today, This Week, This Month, Favorites

### 📦 AI MODEL STORE
Download only the language models you need. Pause, resume, and verify downloads with SHA-256 integrity checks. See exactly how much storage each model uses.

• Smart recommendations based on your device's RAM and CPU
• One-tap model updates when available
• Detailed metadata: name, version, size, quality (1–5★), RAM/CPU needs, license
• Categories: Translation, Voice, Speech Recognition
• Resume interrupted downloads from last byte position

### 🔒 PRIVACY BY DESIGN
• Zero data collection — analytics disabled by default, requires explicit opt-in
• No cloud processing — all AI runs on-device, always
• Offline mode blocks ALL outbound network requests
• No tracking, no profiling, no behavioral analytics
• Only a single interstitial ad on app close (never during use)
• Remove all ads permanently with one-time $20 "Buy Me a Coffee" purchase
• Your data never leaves your device

### ⚡ PERFORMANCE
• Translation in under 300ms (500 chars, model loaded)
• Speech recognition partial results in 200–500ms
• TTS playback begins within 500ms
• Model cold-load under 3 seconds (up to 500MB)
• Battery-aware profiles: Saver, Balanced, Max Performance
• Configurable hardware acceleration: NNAPI, GPU, CPU fallback
• Smart memory management — unused models auto-unload after 5 min

### ♿ ACCESSIBLE
• WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliant
• Screen reader support with semantic roles and state announcements
• System text scaling up to 200% without layout breaks
• Full keyboard navigation on desktop with visible focus indicators
• High contrast mode (4.5:1 normal text, 3:1 large text)
• Haptic feedback per category (configurable)
• Reduced motion preference respected — animations replaced with static states
