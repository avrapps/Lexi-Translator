Translate Anywhere, Without the Cloud

Lexi is a privacy-first, offline AI translation platform. Real-time interpretation, text translation, and natural speech synthesis — all running on-device with no data ever leaving your phone or computer.

Get Lexi Contribute
Capabilities

Everything On-Device

🎙️

Live Interpreter

Real-time dual-language interpretation with animated AI Orb visualization. Speak naturally and get instant translations with configurable silence detection.

📝

Text Translation

Translate text with OPUS-MT neural models. Get alternatives, dictionary entries, transliteration, and confidence scores — all processed locally.

🔊

Neural Speak (TTS)

Convert text and documents to natural speech using Kokoro, Piper, and VITS engines. Adjustable speed, pitch, and multiple voice profiles.

📚

Encrypted Library

All history encrypted with AES-256. Full-text search, folders, tags, and export to JSON, CSV, PDF, or audio formats.

⚙️

Model Management

Browse and download translation, STT, and TTS models. Pause/resume downloads, SHA-256 integrity verification, and smart recommendations.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

Zero network calls for core features. No analytics by default. All processing happens on your device — your data stays yours.
